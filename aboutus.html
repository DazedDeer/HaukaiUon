About Us




The Owners

Welcome, everyone! Haukai is a small restaurant based in New Zealand, in the Bay of Islands. It is owned by us, Steve & Jane, we are a young couple with a real passion for Māori and Pākehā kai. After Jane finished her masters in Te Reo Māori we both realized that there weren't any near by restaurants that offer Māori kai blessed by karakia. This had to change, so we both came to the conclusion that we had to open a restaurant! In 2020, we opened Haukai, it was a humble beginning with up to 20 customers a day. Later on, in 2021 we built our own marae outside the restaurant, this was a huge hit and became a sought after venue for good kai (food) and as a location to hui (to meet). We are always trying to improve our restaurant to make the experience the best way for locals and tourists alike to have a taste of New Zealand culture. Everything from the decor, our language, menu, and marae are tailors especially for this purporse.

Please feel free to browse our menus or make a booking!. We also love to host events and weddings, if you have a group or an event larger than ten people, please contact us. We can accomodate large bookings in our marae.


The Haukai Experience

At Haukai we incorportate principles of Te Ao Māori (the Māori World), this includes using karakia to bless the food for our guests. All our staff have qualifications in Te Reo Māori so we are thrilled to be able to take bookings in Te Reo Māori as well as English.


Our Karakia

Whakapaigna ēnei kai

Hei oranga mō te tinana

Mō ō mātou wairua hoki

Āmine

Bless these foods

for the goodness of our bodies

And for our spirits as well

Amen
